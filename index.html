    • Background

  • Peter Kütt was born in Würzburg in 1956
  • Leistungskurs Musik (music set) with Professor Dr. Lenz Meierott
  • Abitur
  • classical piano and flute studies in Würzburg with Professors Leni Geissler, Wolfgang Arnold and Rybert Mynter National teaching qualification exam (Professor Bertold Hummel)
  • then studies in Graz/Austria,
  • jazz studies at the „academy for music and performing arts“ with Dr. Alfons Dauer, Dr. Dieter Glawischnig and Dr. Harald Neuwirth
  • After first diploma in main subject „jazz piano“,
  • granted admission to Professor Heinz Czadek’s „jazz theory, jazz composition and arrangement“ class
  • main diploma in 1987.
  • Since then, he has lived and worked in Munich, both as a teacher and playing concerts with various big bands in the South of Germany.
  • You can reach him anytime at peter.kuett@me.com
